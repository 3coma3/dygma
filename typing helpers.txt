Writing helper layers

Caps Lock modes

Normal caps lock: press caps lock
Extended caps lock: press caps lock twice
    - word caps: press twice any of comma, colon/semicolon, dot, tab, space, enter
    - sentence caps: press twice any of dot, enter
    - paragraph caps: press twice enter

Punctuation shortcuts
hold L4 + comma: insert space automatically
hold L4 + dot: insert space automatically, capitalize first letter that occurs

Aphostrophe abbreviations

hold L4 + i(I, double tap it), y(you), s(she), h(he), w(we), t(they), '(aphostrophe): enter abbreviation mode
in abbreviation mode, hit l('ll), d('d), r('re), s('s), m('m) to complete, add a space and finish abbreviation


To abort any helper hit any T8 (unconditional lock Home)
